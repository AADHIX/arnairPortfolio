Mechanical Design Project

Advanced mechanical system design with CAD modeling and performance optimization

Project desc

This project involved the design and optimization of a mechanical system for industrial applications. The goal was to improve efficiency while reducing maintenance requirements and operational costs.

Key Features

  • CAD modeling using SolidWorks and AutoCAD
  • Finite Element Analysis for stress testing
  • Thermodynamic performance optimization
  • Material selection for durability
  • Prototype development and testing

Technical Specifications

The system was designed to operate under extreme conditions with temperatures ranging from -20°C to 120°C. The final design achieved a 23% improvement in energy efficiency compared to previous models.
